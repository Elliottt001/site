Co-first author on a research project addressing how to balance **local detail precision** and **long-range dependency memory** in large-scale sequence and vision modeling under a constrained compute budget. We propose an **attention-free hybrid architecture** unifying the efficiency of SSMs with the fine-grained feature capacity of Transformers, reducing O(n²) complexity to near-linear, achieving **SOTA** on multiple benchmarks.
